Delete your account

We understand you may no longer need CalorIA Scan. Before continuing, please read carefully what will happen to your data.

What gets deleted

  • Your profile and personal data (name, email, settings)
  • Your entire history of meals and scans
  • Your macros, goals, and weekly statistics
  • Your favorites list and frequent foods
  • Any active Pro subscription tied to the account

How to delete from inside the app

1
Open CalorIA Scan Go to the main screen of the app on your device.
2
Go to My Profile Tap the profile icon in the bottom bar.
3
Settings → Account Tap the settings icon in the top-right corner.
4
Select "Delete account" Confirm your decision. The process is immediate and irreversible.
This action is permanent and irreversible. Once your account is deleted, we will not be able to recover any of your data. If you have an active Pro subscription, we recommend cancelling it first from the App Store.
