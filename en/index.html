Tecnologia con sabor mexicano

Other apps don't know what chilaquiles con cecina are. We do.

📸

AI Scanning

Take a photo of your food and our AI automatically identifies Mexican dishes, their ingredients, and nutritional values with 98% accuracy.

🌮

Mexican Food Database

Over 600 Mexican dishes registered, from tacos al pastor to mole poblano, with nutritional information verified by nutritionists.

🧩

Customize Ingredients

Adjust each ingredient in your dish: remove the sour cream, add avocado, change the tortilla type. Macros recalculate instantly.

📊

Macro Pills

Visualize proteins, carbs, and fats with intuitive color indicators. Track your daily and weekly progress with clear charts.

📄

Export PDF Report

Generate a PDF report with your meal history, macros, and calories ready to share with your nutritionist. Clear and professional information.

🎯

Personalized Goals

Set calorie and macronutrient goals based on your body, physical activity, and personal goals. The app adapts to you.

🍎

Expanded nutrition NEW

"Today's nutrition" screen redesigned with 4 sections: macros, sugars & fiber, detailed fats, and electrolytes. 6 new nutrients tracked (cholesterol, trans fats, calcium, iron, potassium, net carbs). Iron goal personalized by sex (18mg women, 8mg men) based on NIH RDA — built for the real Mexican diet.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about the 7-day free trial

How does the 7-day free trial work?

When you subscribe to Pro for the first time, you get 7 full days with all features at no charge. Apple charges you automatically on day 7 unless you cancel beforehand. Cancel anytime from Settings > [your name] > Subscriptions on iOS.

Do I need to add a card to start the trial?

Yes, Apple requires a payment method on your Apple ID to start the trial. We don't charge you anything during the 7 days — the charge only happens on day 7 if you decide to continue.

Who is eligible for the trial?

Any new Pro user who has never used a CalorIA Pro promotion before. Apple controls this automatically via your Apple ID.
